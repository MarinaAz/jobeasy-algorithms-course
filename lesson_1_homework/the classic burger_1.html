The Classic Burger

  1. 1 pound ground lean (8% fat) beef
  2. 1 large egg
  3. 1/2 cap minced onion
  4. 1/4 cup fine dried bread crumbs
  5. 1 tbs Worcestershire
  6. 1 or 2 cloves garlic,peeled and minced
  7. about 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. about 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  9. 4 hamburger buns(4 in. wide), split
  10. about 1/4 cup of mayonnaise

Directions

  • Step 1

    • In a bowl mix ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until well blended. Divide mixture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide.

  • Step 2

    • Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue grill over a solid bed of hot coats or high heat on a gas grill (you can hold your hand at grill level only 2 or 3 seconds); close lid on gas grill. Cook burgers, turning once, until browned on both sides and no longer pink inside (cut to test), 7 to 8 minutes total.

  • Step 3

    • Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.

  • Step 4

    • Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms, Add letttuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place.
